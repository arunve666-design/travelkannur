Kavvayi is the second largest backwater system in Kerala, yet far less known than Alleppey. This is exactly what makes it special ‚Äî pristine, peaceful, and completely unspoiled by mass tourism.

The backwaters cover a vast area of interconnected lakes, rivers and canals surrounded by paddy fields, coconut groves and small island villages. It's Kerala at its most authentic.

A boat ride through Kavvayi at dawn, with mist rising off the water and birds calling from the mangroves, is one of the most magical experiences in all of North Kerala.

Why Kannur?

Kannur ‚Äî also known as Cannanore ‚Äî is the cultural capital of North Kerala. It sits on the Malabar Coast and has been a centre of trade, art and tradition for over a thousand years.

It is home to Theyyam ‚Äî one of the world's oldest living ritual art forms, performed in hundreds of temples across the district every year between November and May. Watching a Theyyam is one of the most powerful cultural experiences available anywhere in India.

Beyond Theyyam, Kannur has Asia's longest drive-in beach at Muzhappilangad, the serene Kavvayi backwaters, dramatic hill stations and the incredible Malabar cuisine that food lovers travel across India to taste.

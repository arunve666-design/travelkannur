Welcome to TravelKannur.in. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you visit TravelKannur.in:

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address and message. This information is sent to us via Formspree and used only to respond to your enquiry.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how you use our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages and browser type. This is collected through Google Analytics if enabled.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to serve relevant advertisements.

2. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Affiliate Links

TravelKannur.in participates in affiliate programmes including Booking.com and MakeMyTrip. When you click on these links and make a booking, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend services that we genuinely believe are useful for travellers visiting Kannur.

4. Third Party Links

Our website contains links to external websites including news sources, booking platforms and tourism resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any website you visit.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To analyse website traffic and performance

6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors use our website
  • Functional cookies: Used to remember your preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our website.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our website. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. Children's Privacy

TravelKannur.in is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
